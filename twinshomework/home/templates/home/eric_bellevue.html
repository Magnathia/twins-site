Here are some facts about Bellevue College

  • Bellevue College was founded in January 1966.
  • It is the 3rd largest institution of higher learning in Washington state.
  • As of 2017, more than 460,000 people have taken a class at Bellevue College, and
    58,515 students have earned 50,562 degrees, certificates, and other awards since its founding in 1966.
  • Bellevue College has 18 buildings, including 12 academic buildings and one
    parking garage, with more than 813,309 square feet of space. Of that, 742,784 square
    feet is dedicated classroom/instructional space.
  • The 100 acre campus includes a planetarium, gymnasium, fitness center, art gallery,
    library, cafeteria, coffee shops, theater, and a variety of common areas.
  • Bellevue College is known as the Bulldogs.
